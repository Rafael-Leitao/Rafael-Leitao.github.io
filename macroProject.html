    • The "Macro Spending Analyzer" is a comprehensive financial management application aimed at providing users with a detailed understanding of their spending patterns and financial status. By categorizing expenses and income, users can visualize their financial activity through various interactive charts and graphs, making it easier to assess and optimize their financial decisions.

    In addition to transaction management, the application features a unique analytical component that aggregates spending by categories such as food, utilities, and entertainment, offering users a granular view of where their money goes. This functionality is especially useful for budget planning and tracking financial goals.

    Technically, the "Macro Spending Analyzer" is developed using SwiftUI for its front-end, providing a responsive and aesthetically pleasing user interface that enhances user experience on iOS devices. The backend is powered by SQLite, a choice that ensures lightweight, yet powerful data management capabilities suitable for handling complex queries and large datasets effectively. The application leverages SQLite's robust features to perform data operations like CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) on financial records stored locally on the user’s device.

    On the backend, financial data is structured across several tables such as purchases, expenses, incomes, and balances, allowing for efficient data retrieval and manipulation. The application includes functionalities to automatically update monthly incomes and expenses, reflecting these in user-visible charts and summaries dynamically. Using advanced SQL queries, the app calculates totals and categorizes spending, which are then rendered in real-time through SwiftUI’s powerful visualization capabilities such as pie charts, bar graphs, and line markers, providing users with immediate feedback on their financial health.
